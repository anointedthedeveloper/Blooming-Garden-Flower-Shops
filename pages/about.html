About Blooming Garden

Our story, mission, and the passionate team behind our beautiful creations

Our Story

Blooming Garden began in 2010 with a simple vision: to bring the beauty and joy of fresh flowers to our community. What started as a small family-owned shop has blossomed into a beloved local florist known for our creative arrangements and exceptional service.

Our founder, Emily Wilson, grew up surrounded by her grandmother's beautiful garden. That early exposure to the wonder of flowers inspired a lifelong passion. After studying horticulture and floral design, she opened Blooming Garden with the mission of sharing that same sense of wonder with others.

Over the years, we've had the privilege of being part of countless special moments in our customers' lives - from weddings and anniversaries to birthdays and expressions of sympathy. Each arrangement we create is crafted with care, attention to detail, and a deep appreciation for the natural beauty of flowers.

Our Mission

To enhance life's special moments through the beauty of fresh flowers, providing exceptional quality, creative designs, and outstanding customer service that exceeds expectations.

Our Vision

To become the leading floral destination in our region, known for innovation in design, sustainable practices, and creating meaningful connections through the universal language of flowers.

Our Values

Quality, creativity, sustainability, and community. We believe in sourcing ethically, reducing waste, and giving back to the community that has supported our growth.
